#sprintweek

The goal

To have a platform that: can be customised to the content and timescales associated with the delivery intent; promotes internal engagement; can be monitored, reported and understood; can be reused.

The prototype

On Wednesday of #sprintweek the decision was made to focus the prototype for testing with front-line staff:

  • How would they interact with the platform?
  • Could they be given ownship of their own tasks?
  • Could we introduce future patterns as part of their learning?
